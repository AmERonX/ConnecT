How it works

From idea to team in three steps

No scrolling through profiles. Describe your project — the AI does the rest.

1

Describe your project

Fill in your problem, approach, and idea. Our AI reviews it and generates a clean, normalised summary — you approve it before anything is saved.

2

Get matched instantly

The moment you save your idea, our embedding pipeline finds the closest matches from all active projects — ranked by similarity.

3

Connect and build

Browse ranked match cards, view their ideas, send a connection request. If both accept, a team is formed instantly.

Features

Designed for serious builders

Every feature exists for one reason: getting the right people into the same room.

🧠
AI canonicalization
Your raw idea is processed by an LLM that extracts only what you actually said — no hallucinations, no fluff. You review and approve the summary.
🎯
Semantic matching
Cohere's embedding model turns your idea into a 1024-dimension vector. Matches are ranked by cosine similarity — not keywords.
✅
Live pipeline status
Every idea has a freshness indicator — computing, partial, fresh. You always know if your match list is up to date.
🔒
Nothing stored without your approval
The AI conversation is stateless. Your idea only hits the database once you've read and approved the canonical summary.
