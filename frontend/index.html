Product direction

A calmer, sharper collaboration experience.

The redesign treats ConnecT as a focused product for student builders: editorial spacing, neutral surfaces, one strong accent, and layouts that privilege decisions over decoration.

01

Idea-led onboarding

The first meaningful action is describing the project clearly, so the product immediately understands intent and direction.

02

Readable information hierarchy

Large headings, generous spacing, and quiet surfaces keep dense collaboration data easy to scan on every screen size.

03

Fewer bright signals, better emphasis

Muted neutrals and a restrained teal-blue accent make states feel premium and intentional instead of noisy.

How it works

Three steps from raw idea to active team.

The workflow is simple on purpose. Every step has one job: clarify the idea, confirm it, then move into connection and delivery.

1

Capture the idea

Write the problem, your proposed solution, and how you expect the project to work. Tags and commitment keep the brief realistic.

2

Review the AI summary

The system extracts a canonical summary. If the idea is vague, you get targeted feedback instead of a low-quality save.

3

Connect and form teams

Browse ranked matches, send requests, accept a connection, and move directly into a team view with visible member context.
